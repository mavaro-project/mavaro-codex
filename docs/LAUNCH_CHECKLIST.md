# Mavaro Pilot Launch Checklist

## Goal

Validate whether recurring small-group circles create real repeat interest among people who live, work, or gather near each other.

## Week 0: Setup

- Deploy landing page to mavaro.org.
- Connect Supabase for pilot lead capture.
- Pick one pilot community: apartment building, coworking space, gym, or employer.
- Recruit 50-100 members into onboarding.
- Pick 3-5 partner venues or spaces.

## Week 1: First circle formation

- Export onboarding responses.
- Generate proposed circles in admin.
- Manually review groups.
- Create 6-week schedule.
- Send invites and calendar holds.

## Week 2-7: Run the rhythm

Suggested six-week format:

1. Walk + coffee / aperitivo
2. Sunday Reset / admin night
3. Dinner at a partner venue
4. Fitness or wellness ritual
5. Culture night / gallery / film / conversation
6. Bring-one-friend closing mixer

## Metrics to track

- Onboarding completion rate
- Circle assignment rate
- First-event attendance rate
- Second-event attendance rate
- Average event rating
- Repeat interest: who would meet again
- Unprompted follow-up: did members make plans outside Mavaro?
- Admin/buyer willingness to pay

## Decision rule

Continue if:

- 50%+ attend at least one event
- 30%+ attend two or more events
- 50%+ select at least one person they would meet again
- Pilot buyer agrees that Mavaro improves community feel
